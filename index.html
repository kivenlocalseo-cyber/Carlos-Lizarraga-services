Understanding the Role of a Residential Realtor

A residential realtor specializes in helping clients buy or sell homes for personal use. They understand local market trends, property values, and buyer behaviors. Unlike commercial realtors, who focus on business properties, residential realtors provide personalized attention and guidance throughout every step of the home buying or selling process. Their expertise ensures that your investment decisions are informed and secure.
